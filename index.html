If you're a company with premises in the UK you have a legal obligation to ensure that your electrical equipment is safe for your staff and the public.

You need to keep records and periodically inspect your items. This service will:

  • tell you what the law requires
  • keep you informed about any changes to the rules
  • supply you with customised labels to your company
  • enable you to catalogue your assets
  • automatically remind you when an inspection is due,
  • put you in touch with a directory of local experts

Most faults can be detected visually by the user, and in a normal office there should be regular user checks for damaged equipment. Much equipment should be formally inspected on a schedule, and PAT tested on a less frequent schedule.

Patcie allows you to keep track of the schedule during your 6 monthly user checks and decide when you need to call in a professional for formal visual inspections. by logging in to the ptcie web site, your competent person will know exactly what work has been carried out and what is required to be done next
